Privacy Policy for DreamTrade

Last updated: 22 November 2025

DreamTrade (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is a simulated cryptocurrency trading application designed for educational purposes only. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using DreamTrade, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following information when you register or use the App:

  • Email address (via Firebase Authentication)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • App usage data
  • Crash logs (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Analytics data (via Firebase Analytics, if enabled)

1.3 In-App Data

To support paper-trading features, we may store:

  • Virtual portfolio details
  • Trade history
  • Watchlist data

This data is stored locally using Room Database and may also be synced securely with Firebase if required.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Displaying real-time crypto price data
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Debugging and resolving technical issues
  • Providing customer support
  • Securing the app and preventing fraud

We do NOT use your information for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services Used

DreamTrade uses the following third-party services, which may collect information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Cryptocurrency price API provider (used only to fetch public price data)

We do not share your personal information with any unrelated third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transfer (HTTPS)
  • Secure Firebase authentication
  • Local data stored using Room with proper access controls

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account data
  • Request a copy of the data we hold
  • Update or correct your personal information

You can contact us anytime for these requests.

6. Children’s Privacy

DreamTrade is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any major changes within the App or on this page.
